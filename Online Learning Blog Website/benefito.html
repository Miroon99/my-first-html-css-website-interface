Reduced cost

Online class will surely cut the cost as we do not have to pay for the costs such as transport costs and learning materials.

Flexibility

Students have the flexibility to be in their classes or to do their work as physical class will not be necessary.

Self-discipline and Responsibility

Since online classes require the students to be independent on their own, it will boost their self discipline and responsibility.

Customize learning environment

Students get to customize their own learning environment and space that fit their own comfort.
